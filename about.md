# Endscreen Totals 

## By [ZSA](user:6311498) and Arlind-dev

Options to display total attempts, jumps, and time played on the level endscreen.

Can use Deathlink data to provide total attempts for all linked levels.
Total time is only available with Deathlink enabled.
